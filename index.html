About My Home City--> Taichung

Taichung is the second largest city; Taichung enjoys a mild year-round climate with an comfortable temperature. It also has a highly developed industrial and commercial base, friendly people, and beautiful scenery, all of which contribute to its status as an international-class city. Centrally located in the western half of Taiwan. The city first rose as a major political, economic, transportation, due highway, railway, and shipping port development. There are also a number of department stores and distinctive shopping areas and boutiques. The European-style Jingming Shopping District and Fenglin Restaurant at the Art Garden impart Taichung with a fashionable elegance.
